Banda Aceh

Banda Aceh is the municipality and capital of the province Aceh, the northernmost province on the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. As the center of government, Banda Aceh is the center of economic, political, social and cultural activities. The city of Banda Aceh is also the oldest Islamic city in Southeast Asia , where the city of Banda Aceh is the capital of the Sultanate of Aceh. The city is surrounded by mountains and seas. It has one volcanic mountain, Mt. Seulawah, which is located in the southeast of the city. The stretched over from the east to the west of the city. Located between these two geography, Banda Aceh has a lot of tourist destination, cuisines influenced by the seas and mountain, and a warm weather.
